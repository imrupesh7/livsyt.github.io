BENEFITS OF OUR SERVICES

ROBUST PROCESS IMPLEMENTATION

livSYT’s self-configurable integrated process workflow allows you to digitize
on-site construction processes, thereby shifting construction from memory
dependant operations to system-assisted operations to deliver robust process
implementation across construction lifecycle.

SEAMLESS RESOURCE MOBILIZATION

livSYT automatically allocates activities to your team in real-time thereby significantly reducing the time spent on extensive communication & coordination across different
mediums (calls, SMS, chats, meetings etc.) for people alignment while enhancing
people accountability. Our system-driven approach enables seamless mobilization & synchronized orchestration of resources leading to improved
on-site output.

REAL-TIME HOLISTIC VISIBILITY

livSYT offers you real-time project status visibility right from overall level to the micro-activity level, along with analytics such as time taken to complete activities and checkpoints, dynamic project status, analysis of defects, etc. through our intuitive dashboards and system-generated reports.
